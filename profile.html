Welcome

I am a Post-Doctoral Researcher at the Department of Electronic Engineering at Tsinghua University, adviced by Professor Yu Wang and Professor Yuan Shen. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from Department of Electronic Engineering at Tsinghua University in 2021, adviced by Professor Yuan Shen. Prior to that, I received my B.E. degree in Information Engineering from Xidian University in 2016.

My research interests include statiscal inference, optimization, and information theory. Specifically, I am interested in the following topics: signal processing, network localization, and multi-agent systems.
